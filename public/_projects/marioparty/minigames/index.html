What do you call a party without games?

Trick question! If it doesn’t have games, it’s not a party.

Featured minigames

Not sure what to play first?
Here are a few minigames to get the party started!

Trike Harder

Race on a teeny-tiny trike and pedal like mad in a short race to the finish line!

Sizzling Stakes

Cook every surface of the cube! When a surface is fully cooked, the Joy-Con controller will rumble.

Slaparazzi

The more central you are in the photo, the more points you get. Do whatever you must to be the center of attention!

And so much more!

There are a lot of minigames. Like, a LOT a lot. You can try your hand at different play styles, too!

Master the minigames

Discover 80 new skill-based minigames with a variety of challenges that use the Joy-Con™ controllers in different ways. Plus, there are four additional minigames exclusive to Toad’s Rec Room!
