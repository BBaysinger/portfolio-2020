Data Legend for Gaming Device

1 email (no attachment)
20KB

1 hour surfing the web
15MB

1 min. of streaming video (HD)
5.1MB (306MB/hr)

1 email (with standard attachment)
300KB

1 social media post with photo
350KB

1 app/game/song downloaded
4MB

1 min. of streaming music
500KB (30MB/hr)

1 min. of streaming video (standard)
2MB (120MB/hr)

Note: Data usage varies by device. The above examples are based on averages and are estimates only.
The actual amount of data used for the described activity can vary.
