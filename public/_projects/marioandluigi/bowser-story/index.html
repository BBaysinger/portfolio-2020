No more Mr. Nice Bowser

It’s a classic tale: Boy eats “Lucky Shroom.” Boy inhales his enemies. Boy’s enemies must find a way out, obtain the Star Cure to cure the Blorbs, and help stop Fawful from taking over boy’s castle.

It comes without warning! First, you balloon to blimp size! Then the rolling! You roll away!


It seems to be incurable…or is it?
